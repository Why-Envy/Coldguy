Marine

Deep Sea Trawler Upgrade

Tema Fishing Harbour

The Challenge: Highly corrosive salt air severely degraded the existing blast freezer coils, leading to slow freezing times (12+ hours) and compromised export quality.

The Solution: Extracted the failing units and installed custom marine-grade condenser coils featuring epoxy anti-corrosion coating and upgraded expansion valves.

Result: Blast freeze time reduced to 7 hours, securing premium export grading.

Agricultural

50-Ton Storage Facility

Accra Plains

The Challenge: Client experienced a devastating 15% post-harvest loss due to inadequate temperature stabilization during peak ambient heat periods exceeding 35°C.

The Solution: Engineered and commissioned a custom walk-in cold room utilizing 100mm PU panels and a dual-compressor Bitzer system to guarantee redundancy.

Result: Stabilized core temp at 2°C, cutting product spoilage to <1%.

Industrial

Injection Molding Overhaul

Heavy Industrial Area, Tema

The Challenge: An existing water-cooled chiller was failing repeatedly due to rapid scaling and poor condenser water quality, entirely halting the factory production line.

The Solution: Replaced the failing units with a robust air-cooled industrial chiller system. Integrated advanced particle filtration on the process loop.

Result: Zero unplanned downtime recorded over 14 months of continuous operation.

Medical

Hospital Mortuary Upgrade

Kumasi Metropolis

The Challenge: The medical facility required an absolute fail-safe cooling system for a new 12-body storage unit, demanding strict compliance with sanitation regulations.

The Solution: Designed a complete 304 stainless steel modular unit featuring an automated redundant backup loop that engages instantly upon primary compressor failure.

Result: Achieved 100% uptime compliance with Ministry of Health thermal guidelines.

Commercial

Supermarket Display Retrofit

East Legon, Accra

The Challenge: A major retail outlet was experiencing massive energy spikes and inconsistent cooling across their open-display dairy and meat refrigeration units.

The Solution: Conducted a complete thermal audit. Replaced legacy R-22 compressors with variable speed, high-efficiency scroll compressors and upgraded the digital PID controllers.

Result: Electrical load reduced by 22%, stabilizing display temperatures universally.
