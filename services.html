Commercial Cold Rooms

We design, construct, and commission custom-built walk-in refrigeration and freezer rooms. Essential for agriculture, hospitality, and pharmaceutical storage in the harsh coastal climate.

Industrial Chillers

ColdMan engineers robust industrial water and air-cooled chiller systems tailored for manufacturing and processing plants. We calculate exact thermal loads to prevent system overexertion and reduce overhead electrical costs.

Our approach guarantees flow rate optimization and utilizes advanced filtration on the process loop to prevent condenser scaling.

Marine & Vessel Freezers

Operating out of Tema, we intimately understand the harsh demands of the maritime environment. Specialized marine refrigeration systems are built to endure highly corrosive coastal air, constant vibration, and continuous operation at sea.

Anti-Corrosion Tech

Condenser coils treated with marine-grade epoxy to prevent rapid degradation from salt air.

Blast Freezers

High-velocity air systems designed for immediate fish catch preservation directly on the trawler.

Compliance
