Forged in the Industrial Hub

Headquartered in the heavy industrial enclave of Tema, ColdMan Refrigeration was established to bridge a critical gap: the difference between basic commercial HVAC maintenance and highly specialized, heavy-duty industrial cooling engineering.

The local climate presents extreme thermal challenges. Standard commercial refrigeration units fail rapidly under high ambient heat, extreme humidity, and the highly corrosive salt air of the coast. We recognized that businesses were hemorrhaging capital on repeated compressor failures and product spoilage.

We do not just replace parts; we re-engineer the system to survive the environment.
