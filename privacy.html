Privacy Policy

This policy explains how ColdMan Refrigeration collects, uses, protects, and stores information from our website visitors, customers, and service partners.

How we protect and manage personal data

ColdMan Refrigeration is committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of information entrusted to us by our clients and site visitors.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to provide quotations, perform maintenance, respond to inquiries, and improve our services. This may include:

  • Contact details such as name, phone number, email address, and company name.
  • Project descriptions, service requirements, and any technical details you provide through our forms.
  • Device and website usage data, including browser type, IP address, and pages viewed.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to service requests and schedule maintenance visits.
  • Deliver quotes, invoices, and technical documentation.
  • Improve website performance, content, and user experience.
  • Communicate updates about emergency response capacity, new services, and safety notices when we have your consent.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with third parties only when it is necessary to support our services and only under strict confidentiality arrangements.

Examples include:

  • Service partners who assist with logistics, technical support, or equipment procurement.
  • Government agencies or law enforcement where disclosure is required by law.
  • Compliance and auditing providers when we conduct internal or external reviews.

4. Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or destruction.

Our measures include:

  • Secure servers and encrypted form transmissions.
  • Access controls for staff and third-party service providers.
  • Regular reviews of our security procedures and system monitoring.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance and understand user behavior.

This may include:

  • Session cookies to maintain website functionality.
  • Analytics tools to monitor page visits and improve content.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but some website features may not function correctly if cookies are blocked.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may connect to third-party service providers for analytics, hosting, and communication. These providers are responsible for their own privacy practices.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing personal information.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to the information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccuracies, or request that we delete your data where required by applicable law.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from children without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The effective date will be updated at the top of this page.
